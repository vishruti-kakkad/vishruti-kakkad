Beyond

A few things I do when I am not working.

My first Canvas painting

I paint. Horses, landscapes, abstract work, tulips, whatever I feel like that week. I have been doing this since I was a kid, and it is the thing I come back to when I need to slow down. The painting above is one of mine.

I sketch and draw too. Small pencil studies in notebooks, quick drawings, things I do not always finish. These are the ones I actually carry around with me, since they fit in a bag. Painting is the slow craft. Drawing is what I do in between.

Other things

A short, honest list of what I spend time on outside the screen.

Studio
Canvas painting has been a steady practice of mine since I was a kid, mostly horses, landscapes, and flowers.
Sketchbook
Small pencil sketches, character drawings, the sort of low-stakes work I carry around in a notebook.
Outside
Sunrise hikes when I can manage them, long walks without a destination, and farmer's markets I have not been to yet.
Kitchen
Cooking most nights, Baking on some. I like to work through healthier takes on Indian recipes I grew up with.
Coffee
Exploring new brews and cafés is a hobby I have not yet managed to call a problem.
Travel
I bring back a fridge magnet from everywhere I go, and my fridge is officially running out of room...
Liverpool FC
While, I donot play, I follow the club with more opinions than any of my friends asked for. YNWA.
Community
I volunteer as a mentor at the ThinkCyber Foundation, mostly with students finding their way into security.
